The Best Casinos in New Zealand

New Zealand might not be the first place that comes to mind when thinking of casinos, but it offers a selection of top-notch venues that provide a world-class gaming experience in some of the country’s most vibrant cities. Here’s a look at the best casinos in New Zealand that are worth visiting.

SkyCity Auckland

Location: Auckland

Overview: As the largest casino in New Zealand, SkyCity Auckland is a must-visit for anyone looking for a premier gaming experience. Located in the heart of Auckland, this casino features over 2,100 gaming machines and 150 table games, including blackjack, roulette, and poker. Beyond gaming, SkyCity is also home to some of the city’s finest restaurants, luxury accommodations, and the iconic Sky Tower, offering stunning views of Auckland. Whether you’re a seasoned player or just looking for a night out, SkyCity Auckland has something for everyone.

Christchurch Casino

Location: Christchurch

Overview: Christchurch Casino, New Zealand’s first casino, has been a central part of the city’s entertainment scene since 1994. Located in the heart of Christchurch, this casino offers a wide range of gaming options, including 500 gaming machines and 36 table games. In addition to the gaming floor, Christchurch Casino is known for its elegant dining options, with several restaurants and bars that offer a variety of cuisines and cocktails. The casino also hosts regular events, including live music and poker tournaments, making it a lively spot for both locals and visitors.

SkyCity Hamilton

Location: Hamilton

Overview: Located along the Waikato River, SkyCity Hamilton offers a more intimate gaming experience compared to its Auckland counterpart. This casino features a well-curated selection of gaming machines and table games, along with a poker room that attracts players from around the region. SkyCity Hamilton is also known for its social atmosphere, with several bars and restaurants on-site, including a popular sports bar where guests can watch live sports while enjoying their favorite drinks. The casino’s riverside location adds to its charm, making it a great place to unwind and enjoy a night out.

Dunedin Casino

Location: Dunedin

Overview: Housed in the historic Southern Cross Hotel, Dunedin Casino offers a unique blend of history and modern gaming. This boutique casino is known for its elegant interior, featuring grand staircases, chandeliers, and stained-glass windows that add a touch of old-world charm. Despite its smaller size, Dunedin Casino offers a wide range of gaming options, including blackjack, roulette, and baccarat, along with 180 gaming machines. The casino also features a stylish bar and restaurant, making it a popular spot for both gaming and socializing in the heart of Dunedin.

SkyCity Queenstown

Location: Queenstown

Overview: Queenstown, known as the adventure capital of New Zealand, is also home to one of the country’s most picturesque casinos. SkyCity Queenstown offers a cozy and relaxed gaming environment, with a selection of gaming machines and table games. Located in the heart of Queenstown, the casino is surrounded by stunning mountain and lake views, providing a perfect backdrop for an evening of entertainment. In addition to gaming, the casino’s on-site restaurant and bar offer a great place to enjoy a meal or a drink after a day of exploring Queenstown’s outdoor attractions.
